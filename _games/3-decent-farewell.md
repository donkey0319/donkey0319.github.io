## Overview

A Decent Farewell is a VR game where players playing as an undertaker decorating the corpse and gradually unveiling the mystery behind the corpse.

![Decent Farewell screenshot 1](/assets/images/decentfarewell/farewell7.png)

![Decent Farewell screenshot 2](/assets/images/decentfarewell/farewell6.png)

The main mechanic is to grab the right objects (clothes) and put it on the corpse. If the player puts on the wrong clothes to the corpse, some horror events will happen. After all clothes and ornaments are put on correctly, the player will reach to the ending of the game and finally learn the story behind.

![Decent Farewell screenshot 3](/assets/images/decentfarewell/farewell3.png)

## In-person Showcase

This game is one of the assignments from the course Building Virtual Worlds from Entertainment Technology Center in Carnegie Mellon University. This game was successfully selected to the Festival, which is a final presentation of the course open to the public. We have decorated the room to match the horro atmosphere of our game.

![Decent Farewell playroom ](/assets/images/decentfarewell/farewell4.png)
