## Overview

![Granny's Gift screenshot 2](/assets/images/grannygift/grannygift_3.png)

Granny's Gift is a game where players have to wrap as many gifts as possible in a given amount of time. Players can choose three types of wrapping paper, some of them is hard to wrap while others are relatively easy. The hard wrapping papers give more scores than the easy ones.

![Granny's Gift screenshot 3](/assets/images/grannygift/grannygift_2.png)

To wrap gifts, players have to hit the buttons in the correct order indicated from the screen.
