## Gameplay Video

{% include video id="67u-HdfUOy0" provider="youtube" %}

The two players are controlling the planes through an accelerometer. The planes in the game will follow the rotation of the accelerometer so that the players can steer the planes by tilting the accelerometer.

![Ginger Air screenshot 1](/assets/images/gingerair/ginger3.png)

There is also a button on the accelerometer which allows the player to punch the other plane such that the gift carrying on the other player will drop.

![Ginger Air screenshot 2](/assets/images/gingerair/ginger4.png)
