## Overview

This is a game from the Global Game Jam in 2026, where the subject is MASK.

![MaskEXE screenshot 1](/assets/images/maskexe/mask1.png)

We designed a puzzle in which the players have to fill the area with blocks. If there are blocks that exceed the area, the players have to flip the blocks to MASK off the extra in order to proceed to the next level.

![MaskEXE screenshot 1](/assets/images/maskexe/mask2.png)
