## Overview

The four players are controlling the penguin through an arcade device called Jam-O-Drum. There is a wheel and a drum at the four corners, where players rotate the wheel to steer the penguin and hit the drum to kick other penguins.

![Kung-Fu Penguin screenshot 1](/assets/images/kungfupenguin/drum.png)

The players goal is to bring as many stones as possible back to their house within an amount of time.

![Kung-Fu Penguin screenshot 1](/assets/images/kungfupenguin/penguin2.png)

 However, penguins can kick other penguins to make their stone drop if they're carrying.

![Kung-Fu Penguin screenshot 3](/assets/images/kungfupenguin/penguin3.png)

There are also random events which can knock off penguins from the arena!
